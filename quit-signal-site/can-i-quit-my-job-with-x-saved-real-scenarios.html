Can I Quit My Job With X Saved? Real Scenarios

This is the practical version of the savings question. The answer changes a lot depending on whether X is $100k, $200k, or $300k and what your monthly burn looks like after you leave.

Scenario 1: Lower Savings

If your savings are closer to $100k, you need a relatively low burn or additional income to make the math comfortable. One big expense or insurance premium can change the outcome quickly.

Scenario 2: Middle Savings

At $200k, many people can quit if they are willing to reduce spending and if they do not expect a very long job search.

Scenario 3: Higher Savings

At $300k and above, the decision often becomes less about raw survival and more about timing, bonus, vesting, and how much stress you are willing to tolerate.
