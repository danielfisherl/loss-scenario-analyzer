When Is It Safe to Stop Working?

The safest answer depends on whether your savings can cover your spending, whether you have other income, and how much uncertainty you are willing to accept.

The Safe-Enough Test

  • You have enough runway for at least 12 months
  • Your expected non-job income is realistic, not hopeful
  • Your healthcare plan is already included
  • You know what you will do if a job search takes longer

When It Is Probably Not Safe

If your runway is short, your costs are high, and you do not have a backup plan, then stopping work is more likely to create stress than freedom.

When It Is Safer

If your runway is long, your income needs are low, and the decision is driven more by choice than desperation, stopping work can be reasonable.
