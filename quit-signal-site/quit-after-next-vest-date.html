Should I Quit After My Next Vest Date?

Waiting for the next vest date is often rational when the vest is close and the value is meaningful. The decision is usually about runway, not just the RSU number itself.

Wait If

  • Your vest is within 1 to 3 months
  • The vest value is large relative to your liquid savings
  • Your next job search may take longer than expected
  • You can tolerate staying a little longer without damaging your health

Quit Sooner If

  • Burnout is becoming a real health issue
  • Waiting does not add much financial value
  • Your runway is already strong
  • The role is getting worse, not better

Simple Rule

If the next vest materially improves runway, and you can wait without meaningful downside, waiting is usually the better financial decision. If the vest is small or far away, it should not dominate the call.
