Runway Guide

Runway Calculator for Tech Workers

A runway calculator answers one question: how long can you live on your savings before you need income again? For tech workers, that answer is usually more useful than raw salary or total comp because equity, healthcare, and uneven compensation timing change the result.

What Runway Means

Runway is your savings divided by your net monthly burn after accounting for healthcare, household income, and any planned break income.

What To Include

  • Cash savings
  • Severance after tax
  • Monthly spending after quitting
  • Other household income
  • Healthcare delta
  • One-time quitting costs

What To Ignore

  • Gross salary you no longer receive
  • Unrealized future raises
  • RSUs you have not vested yet
