How Long Can I Live Off $200k Without Income?

Decision memo · Updated March 2026 · Built for the time-off-work calculator

$200k without income can feel like a lot until you test it against the monthly burn of your actual life. That is especially true if housing, healthcare, or family costs are high.

This page is most useful when you want a break but need to know whether the break is calm enough to be useful.

How to read the runway

At moderate spending levels, $200k can buy a long break. At high spending levels, it can still disappear faster than expected, especially if you are trying to preserve a large cushion.

What changes the answer?

  • If you can trim burn before the break, your runway expands dramatically.
  • Part-time income during the break can change the answer more than small investment returns.
  • If you are in a high-cost city, the same savings is simply less forgiving.

Reality check

  • Reality check: the same $200k can feel generous or tight depending on whether your life costs $6k or $14k a month.
  • The right way to think about it is months of margin, not just cash balance.
