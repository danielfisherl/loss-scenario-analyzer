Common Questions

FAQ

How much savings do I need to take a year off?

It depends on monthly burn, healthcare, and whether you want to preserve a buffer. For many people, the useful unit is months of freedom, not a single savings number.

Is this for quitting permanently?

No. This calculator is for a temporary break, sabbatical, or reset. If you are trying to fund retirement, you need a different model.

Does this include healthcare?

Yes. You can enter an additional monthly healthcare cost so the break estimate reflects a real post-employer budget.

How conservative should I be?

Conservative enough that the answer still works if one month runs expensive. For most users, that means keeping a meaningful emergency buffer and not assuming perfect spending.

What if I expect some income during the break?

Put that in the income field. Even modest consulting or freelance work can materially change a 6- or 12-month break.
