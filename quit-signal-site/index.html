How To Read It

How to think about taking time off

If the calculator gives you a strong time-off signal, the decision is mostly about timing and the amount of margin you want to keep. If it says possible but tight or not yet, the next move is usually to lower burn, wait for a payout, or both.

The goal is not certainty. It is to make the tradeoffs visible enough that you can choose without hand-waving.

Quick FAQ

Is this financial advice?

No. It is a structured way to think through readiness, runway, and tradeoffs.

What if I’m burned out but my runway is okay?

That usually shifts the decision toward timing, not just savings. The signal may be strong enough to act, but the right move still depends on your margin.

What if I have a bonus or vest coming soon?

Run both scenarios. Waiting only matters if the payout changes the signal enough to move you from risky to strong, or from not yet to workable.
