$300k Scenario

Can I Quit With $300k Saved?

$300k is often enough to create real flexibility, but the answer still depends on your burn, household income, and near-term vesting or bonus value.

Quick Rule

If your post-quit burn is in the low single digits or low teens, $300k can support a long runway. If your burn is high, the savings disappear faster than it feels like they should.

When $300k Looks Strong

  • You can lower monthly spending after leaving
  • You have a partner or other household income
  • Your healthcare gap is manageable
  • You can wait for a good job search instead of a desperate one

When $300k Still Needs Attention

  • High cost of living city
  • Big mortgage or childcare costs
  • Long job search in a narrow specialty
  • Large unvested RSUs or bonus still on the table
